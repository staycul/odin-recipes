Fufu Recipe

Description

The image above describes Jollof Rice in its full glory after preparation.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomato Paste
  • Dried Fish
  • Goat Meat
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Cook rice and add tomato paste then boil further
  2. Steam goat meat then deep fry in oil
  3. Mix goat meat and dried fish with the rice
  4. Cut vegetables and spread on served rice
  5. Food is ready to be served
