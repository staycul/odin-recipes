Fufu Recipe

Description

The image above describes fufu in its full glory after preparation.

Ingredients

  • Corn dough
  • Cassava dough
  • Tilapia or Salmond
  • Vegetables
  • Shito

Steps

  1. Prepare a mixture of 2 corn dough is to 1 cassava dough
  2. Cook mix whiles stirring continuously with wooden laden
  3. Form the cooked mixture into round balls size of an adult fist
  4. Blend tomato, green pepper, garlic and ginger into a fine paste
  5. Cut onions, carrots and other vegetables into paste
  6. Grill tilapia or salmond
  7. Food is ready to be served
